ABOUT ME

I HAVE LOVED READING BOOKS SINCE I WAS 4 AND NOW I LOVE IT EVEN MORE. I FEEL READING MAKES YOU CREATIVE BECAUSE WHEN YOU READ A BOOK (NOVEL) YOU THINK EVERYTHING FROM THE WRITERS POINT OF VIEW. I REALLY LIKE DETECTIVE NOVELS AND MYTHOLOGY AND YOU WILL NEVER FIND ANY CORNER OF OUR HOUSE WITHOUT A BOOK. SOME OF MY FAVOURITE BOOKS ARE 'THE DIARY OF A YOUNG GIRL' BY ANNE FRANK, 'THE LITTLE PRINCESS' BY FRANCES HODGSON BURNETT. MY FAVOURITE DETECTIVE STORY SERIES IS NANCY DREW.

I LIKE WRITING TOO (BUT NOT SCHOOL NOTES). I PREFER WRITING ON RANDOM TOPICS BECAUSE AS YOU CAN SEE ABOVE I HAVE WRITTEN ABOUT READING A BOOK FROM WRITER'S POINT OF VIEW THAT MEANS WHEN A PERSON IS WRITING THEY USE THEIR CREATIVITY. WHEN I WAS 5 MY MOTHER HAD GIVEN ME A BOOK IN WHICH I USED TO WRITE ON TOPICS GIVEN BY MY MOTHER. I WAS QUITE YOUNG DURING THAT TIME BUT HAD DEVELOPED AN INTEREST IN READING AND WRITING.

I ALSO HAVE AN INTEREST FOR PAINTING AND HAVE MADE A COUPLE OF ART PIECES. TILL NOW YOU MIGHT HAVE UNDERSTOOD MY INTEREST AND LOVE FOR CREATIVE THINGS SO YOU KNOW THE REASON WHY I LIKE PAINTING TOO. I ESPECIALLY LIKE DRAWING MANDALA (A TYPE OF DRAWING WHERE WE MAKE VARIOUS DESIGNS) BECAUSE AS WE MAKE THE SAME DESIGN AGAIN AND AGAIN WE ARE CONCENTRATING OUR MIND INTO THE PIECE WHICH HELPS IN CALMING OUR MIND.

LISTENING TO MUSIC IS ONE OF THE FAVOURITES OF MOST PEOPLE. MY FAVOURITE TYPE OF MUSIC IS K-POP AS I FEEL THAT IT JUST HAS SO MANY DIFFERENT VIBES IN DIFFERENT SONGS. THATS THE REASON WHY SO MANY PEOPLE ARE INTO K-POP.

NOWADAYS EVERYBODY IS STRESSED AND ALL THE ABOVE ACTIVITIES WHICH I HAVE WRITTEN ABOUT ABOVE ACTUALY CALM YOUR MIND. LETS TAKE AN EXAMPLE, JUST THINK YOU HAVE COME FROM WORK/SCHOOL AND YOU ARE EXTREMELY STRESSED. WHAT WOULD YOU LIKE TO DO? -
 
READ A BOOK / SMALL STORY
WRITE ABOUT ALL THE STRESS YOU HAVE/ PROBLEMS YOU HAD IN THE DAY
DRAW ANYTHING / SPLASH SOME PAINT ON THE SHEET
LISTEN TO YOUR FAVOURITE MUSIC.

HONESTLY YOU COULD LIKE TO DO ANYTHING BUT PERSONALLY I WOULD LIKE TO DO EVERYTHING. YOU COULD TRY ANY ONE AND SEE HOW IT HELPS YOU. 

THANK YOU FOR YOUR TIME AND PATIENCE. HOPE YOU KNOW ME A LITTLE BETTER NOW
